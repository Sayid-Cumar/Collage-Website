Welcome To Mogadishu Institute ofTechnology

Mogadishu Institute Of tecnology (MIT) is private institution and was officially established in Mogadishu, Somalia, in 2014 by a group of Somali scholars and Intellectuals to fill the existing vacuum (in the quality) in the higher education system in Somalia by providing higher education of international standards.

We are different and we like it that way. JUST is professionally oriented university.

Our academic staff is not only some of the smartest but passionate about providing students with up to date curriculums and text books and producing experienced, engaged successful graduates.
